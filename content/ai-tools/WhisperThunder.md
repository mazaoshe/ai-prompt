## About Whisper Thunder

Whisper Thunder is an AI-powered video generation tool that transforms static images or text prompts into dynamic, engaging videos. This platform enables content creators, marketers, and artists to produce high-quality video content without the need for complex editing software or extensive technical skills. The AI technology behind Whisper Thunder brings creativity to life with minimal effort.

- Customizable video styles
