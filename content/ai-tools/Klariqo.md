## About Klariqo

Klariqo is an AI-powered assistant designed to handle customer interactions around the clock. It automates both phone calls and website chats, providing businesses with a seamless way to engage with customers without requiring constant human supervision. This solution helps companies improve their customer service efficiency while reducing operational costs.

## Key Features

- 24/7 phone call handling
- Website chat automation
- Intelligent conversation routing
- Multi-channel support
- Automated response generation

- Customer support automation
- Lead qualification and capture
- Appointment scheduling
- FAQ handling
- Order status inquiries
